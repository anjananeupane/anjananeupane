About Nepal

- The Land of the Himalayas

Nepal, officially the Federal Democratic Republic of Nepal, is a landlocked country in South Asia. It is mainly located in the Himalayas, but also includes parts of the Indo-Gangetic Plain. It has diverse geography, including fertile plains, subalpine forested hills, and eight of the world's ten tallest mountains, including Mount Everest, the highest point on Earth. Kathmandu is the nation's capital and the largest city.

Nepal is known for its rich cultural heritage, historical sites, and natural beauty. It is a popular destination for trekking, mountaineering, and adventure tourism. The country is also famous for its festivals, temples, and the friendly hospitality of its people.
